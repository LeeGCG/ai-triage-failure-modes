# Triage Scenarios

## Scenario 1: Clear Emergency
Patient reports chest pain, shortness of breath, and sweating.

## Scenario 2: Ambiguous Case
Patient reports fatigue, mild chest discomfort, and anxiety.

## Scenario 3: Edge Case
Young patient with no history reports sudden severe headache and nausea.

## Scenario 4: Misleading Input
Patient insists symptoms are “just stress” but reports dizziness and blurred vision.

## Scenario 5: Incomplete Information
Patient reports feeling “off” but provides no additional detail.
